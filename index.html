About Me

Hi, I am Nazmul Hasan. I was born in the year 1998, Kishoregonj, Bangladesh. I lived a great life till now and greatful to Almighty for all the blessings.

I passed my secondary and higher secondary education from Sylhet Cadet College. As my college was military based, I lived a adventurous and challanging life there. I am still connected to large portion of the alumni. I am also an executive member of the Old Cadet Associacion of Sylhet(OCAS).

I have completed my BSc in Computer Science from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology(BUET). I am blessed with many helpful senior brothers from BUET. After completion I visited Japan as an intern. While working there I realized my calling and came back to academics. Though I have a penchant for developing, I want to pursue higher studies and continue developing as a hobby. I have worked in various fields of Computer Science. I really enjoy the learning phase. After a while I found the field where I really want to contribute. I possess a profound passion for Natural Language Processing (NLP) and am deeply committed to advancing my expertise in this specialized field. My aspiration is to pursue meaningful opportunities that allow me to contribute significantly to the advancements and applications within the domain of NLP.
